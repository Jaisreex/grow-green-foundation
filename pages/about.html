Who We Are

Grow Green Foundation is a community-driven initiative focused on planting fruit trees in public spaces to create free food sources for people and wildlife.

Our Mission

To plant fruit trees and engage communities in sustainable environmental action.

Planet

We work to improve environmental health and support biodiversity by planting resilient fruit trees in urban areas.

People

Creating free food sources for people through accessible public orchards and community-led planting initiatives.

Wildlife

Our focus on fruit-bearing trees ensures that local urban wildlife and birds have natural food sources to thrive.

Our Core Difference

What Makes Us Different

We don’t just plant trees — we plant trees that produce food for people and animals.

Our Vision

The Future We Build

To create a future where public spaces provide free food, support biodiversity, and improve environmental health. We aim to build a world where every neighborhood is a garden of abundance and nature thrives alongside communities.
