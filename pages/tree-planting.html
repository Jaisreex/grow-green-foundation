Primary Program

Tree Planting Initiative

Restoring Earth, One Fruit Tree at a Time

Our Tree Planting Initiative is the cornerstone of Grow Green Foundation. We don't just plant saplings; we design community orchards that serve as vibrant, self-sustaining food systems for urban and rural neighborhoods alike.

We focus exclusively on fruit-bearing and nutrient-rich indigenous trees that are compatible with the local climate, ensuring high survival rates and maximum community benefit.

  • Ecosystem Restoration: Improving soil health, supporting pollinators, and increasing local biodiversity.
  • Food Security: Creating free, accessible food sources for people who need them most.
  • Climate Mitigation: Naturally sequestering carbon and reducing the 'heat island' effect in concrete-heavy cities.
