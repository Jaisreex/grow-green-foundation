Program I

Tree Plantation

Our mission is to plant resilient, fruit-bearing trees worldwide to eliminate hunger and capture carbon.

Where We Plant

We focus our plantation efforts in rural villages, educational institutions, shared community lands, smallholder farms, and degraded urban environments.

  • Community Food Forests
  • Roadside and Park Orchards
  • Reclamation of degraded lands

Long-term Impact & Benefits

A single mature fruit tree provides decades of free, nutritious food, strengthens soil integration, and creates diverse localized economies through fruit harvest sharing.
